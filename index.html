Equilibrium Is a Death Sentence.

We were taught to chase balance. But perfect balance is not life. It is the end of motion, the loss of usable difference, the place where nothing can choose, change, or become.

If zero were truly possible, reality would not be here to notice it. A system with no differential has no direction. A universe with no asymmetry has no reason to become. Existence implies distinguishability. Distinguishability implies asymmetry.

RAD-PDSA began as a practical stability architecture for adaptive systems. Potentiation expands. Restraint preserves. Q resolves. These are dual actors on one field, not enemies.

Stability is not static balance. Stability is governed asymmetry.
